## Observations

- Product catalog significantly improved product normalization.
- Common grocery items such as Sooji, Olper, and Russ were correctly recognized.
- Quantities and amounts remained accurate.
- Total amount matched the manual calculation.
- One ambiguous product was incorrectly normalized.
- One low-confidence product was still normalized instead of preserving the original OCR text.
