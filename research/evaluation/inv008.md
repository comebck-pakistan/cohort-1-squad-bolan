---

# Overall Assessment

Adding a product catalog substantially improved handwritten product recognition.

Most grocery products were normalized successfully while preserving numerical accuracy.

However, the experiment also demonstrates that product normalization should only occur when confidence is sufficiently high.

---

# Recommendation

Future versions of the prompt should include an explicit confidence threshold such as:

"If confidence is below High, do not normalize the product name. Return the original OCR text instead."

This would reduce incorrect product mappings while maintaining the benefits of the product catalog.
