# Overall Assessment

This sample demonstrates strong OCR and document understanding performance on a WhatsApp-compressed invoice image. Despite the cropped footer, Gemini accurately extracted the structured content, including supplier information, product list, totals, and nearly all visible Urdu text.

The model also demonstrated contextual language understanding by completing the partially visible Urdu sentence. While this behavior is useful for readability, production systems should distinguish between text that is directly observed and text that is inferred.

---

# Conclusion

Gemini 2.5 Pro performed very well on this invoice. All business-critical information was extracted correctly, and the only notable behavior was contextual completion of a partially cropped Urdu footer. This did not impact the accuracy of the extracted invoice data but highlights the importance of clearly separating OCR extraction from language-based inference in production applications.
