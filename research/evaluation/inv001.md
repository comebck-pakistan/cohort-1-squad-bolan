## Observations

- Correctly identified that invoice number was not available.
- Correctly identified that invoice date was not available.
- Correctly returned `null` for payment terms.

### Incorrect

- Failed to identify several visible product names.
- Hallucinated product names that do not match the visible invoice.
- Added products that cannot be confidently verified from the damaged invoice.

### Unverifiable

## Overall Assessment

The model correctly identified that the invoice did not contain a customer name, invoice number, invoice date, or payment terms.

Product extraction was inconsistent. Some products and numerical values were extracted, but several visible product names did not match the invoice. Due to significant water damage, multiple sections of the invoice were partially unreadable, making complete verification impossible.

This invoice represents a challenging edge case rather than a typical invoice. Since portions of the document were difficult to interpret even through manual inspection, some of Gemini's predictions cannot be conclusively classified as correct or incorrect.

Accuracy was not calculated for this sample because several fields could not be reliably verified due to document degradation.

## Conclusion

This invoice should be treated as a challenging edge-case sample because the document quality is severely degraded.

The evaluation shows that document quality has a significant impact on extraction performance. Future evaluations should include a mixture of clear printed invoices, handwritten invoices, and damaged invoices to obtain a balanced assessment of the model's capabilities.

This sample demonstrates that some extraction errors may be caused by poor source quality rather than limitations of the model alone.
