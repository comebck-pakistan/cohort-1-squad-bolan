## Observations

Although Prompt V7 significantly improved structural understanding by detecting crossed-out items and producing the correct invoice total, it continued to normalize partially readable handwritten products to incorrect catalog entries.

The crossed-out products were manually verified as:

- Choco Bunty
- Magic Bunty

Gemini instead produced:

- Choco Paste
- Jeju Wafer

This indicates that the model still exhibits **catalog bias**, where ambiguous handwriting is matched to the closest known product instead of being marked as uncertain.

---

## Recommendation

Future versions should perform OCR and product normalization as separate stages.

Suggested workflow:

1. Extract raw OCR text.
2. Preserve uncertain handwriting.
3. Apply product catalog matching only when OCR confidence is sufficiently high.
4. Return `normalized_product: null` for ambiguous or crossed-out handwritten text instead of forcing a catalog match.

This approach would reduce incorrect product substitutions while maintaining accurate invoice totals.
