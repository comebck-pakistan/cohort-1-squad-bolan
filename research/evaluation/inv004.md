## Overall Assessment

This invoice represents a high-quality printed invoice with a clear layout and complete business information. Gemini successfully extracted all relevant fields, including metadata, customer details, supplier information, product information, promotions, taxes, totals, footer details, and handwritten annotations.

The only deviation from the prompt was the use of the string "Not Available" instead of JSON `null` for fields that were absent from the document.

---

## Conclusion

This sample demonstrates excellent document understanding performance. Compared to previous prompts, Prompt V3 produced a significantly more comprehensive extraction by capturing nearly every visible element on the invoice, making it the strongest prompt for research and evaluation purposes.

For production use, a post-processing step should normalize values such as "Not Available" into JSON `null` to ensure schema consistency.
