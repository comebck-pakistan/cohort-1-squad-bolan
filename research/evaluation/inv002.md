## Overall Assessment

This invoice was significantly clearer than the first evaluation sample. Most fields could be verified through manual inspection, and Gemini extracted the majority of structured information correctly.

Only one product name could not be confidently verified due to unclear handwriting. Since the original text is ambiguous, this extraction cannot be classified as either correct or incorrect.

## Conclusion

This invoice demonstrates strong extraction performance on a relatively readable handwritten invoice. The remaining uncertainties are primarily due to ambiguous handwriting rather than obvious extraction failures by the model.
