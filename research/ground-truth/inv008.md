# Research Findings

This experiment demonstrates that providing a domain-specific product catalog significantly improves handwritten invoice understanding.

Compared with previous experiments:

- Generic OCR produced raw handwritten text.
- Invoice-specific prompting improved field extraction.
- Product catalog prompting improved product normalization.

However, overly aggressive normalization can introduce incorrect product names when handwriting is ambiguous.

A confidence-based normalization strategy is therefore recommended for future iterations.
